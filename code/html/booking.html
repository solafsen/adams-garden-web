Adam's Garden

A place for friends, family and loved ones to meet and share good memories and delicious food. As we are situated in the city center near the harbor promenade, our venue come with a breathtaking ocean view. Imagine enjoying your favorite meal watching the beautiful sunset over the ocean from our cozy restaurant with your favorite people!

Adam's Garden facilities are perfect venues for birthday parties, weddings, Christmas parties or any other event. With a seating capacity of 100 people, a romantic atmosphere with a 180-degree amazing ocean view, a dance floor, a bar, delightful cuisine, and a helpful and friendly staff, you have all the ingredients to make your event a night to remember.
